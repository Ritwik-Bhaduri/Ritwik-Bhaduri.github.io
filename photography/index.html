Welcome to my photography collection!

When I'm not working I enjoy capturing places, moments, and details that stand out to me as I travel. This gallery is a selection of photographs that reflect how I see the world, from landscapes and city scenes to everyday moments along the way.

01

Fall '22 Colors in New Hampshire

A set of photographs from a visit to Lake Winnipesaukee in New Hampshire during the fall of 2022, capturing the changing foliage and the atmosphere of the season.

02

Sunset at the Thar Desert

A set of photographs from Rajasthan in December 2019, capturing the changing light and color of sunset over the Thar Desert.

03

Fall '25 Colors in Vermont

A set of photographs from a visit to Vermont during the fall of 2025, capturing the vibrant foliage and quiet landscapes of a New England autumn.

04

April '24 Solar Eclipse

Photographs from the April 8, 2024 solar eclipse in Newport, Vermont. A rare moment in the sky, with the next total eclipse visible from the United States not arriving until March 30, 2033, in Alaska.

05

Musical Instruments from Poush Mela

Musical instruments from Poush Mela, the annual village fair of Santiniketan in West Bengal, India. With roots dating back to 1894, the mela has celebrated Bengal's folk traditions for over 130 years.

06

New York City

A few photographs of a misty New York City, captured during one of my many visits.
